# Ezi 90 SI Insulated Steel Roller Shutter (Ezi-Roll)

## Summary

Product brochure for the Ezi-Roll Ezi 90 SI insulated steel roller shutter — a twin-wall interlocked steel slat door with a polyurethane foam core, rated at U-value 4.2 W/m²K (R-value 0.24 m²K/W) — aimed at commercial and industrial openings needing thermal or acoustic performance. Useful for assessments needing the thermal rating of an insulated roller/garage-style door (e.g. garage door inputs or sealed shed openings).

## Key claims

- Thermal rating: U-value = 4.2 W/m²K, equivalent R-value = 0.24 m²K/W (stated as the door's "thermal conductivity rating"; no test standard or conditions cited in the brochure).
- Acoustic performance: 20 dB reduction.
- Curtain construction: 90 mm × 23 mm nominal twin-wall steel slat injected with polyurethane foam; interlocking slats with nylon end clips.
- Suits openings up to 6 m high × 8 m wide, with a maximum coverage area of 45 m².
- Guide tracks: 43 mm × 105 mm aluminium with extruded rubber silencing strips both sides of the curtain, plus a lintel seal at the head — both described as assisting reduction of heat transfer at the door perimeter.
- Bottom rail: 23 mm × 100 mm aluminium rectangular hollow section with a 60 mm double-tongued rubber weather seal.
- Electrically operated (direct-drive motor with electronic fall-arrest/lockout brake); optional spring-assisted drum for smaller manually operated doors.
- Standard finish: white enamel; can be powdercoated.
- Warranty: 12 months limited (material, labour and workmanship).
- Manufactured in Australia by Ezi-Roll Doors Australia (steel roller shutters since 1971).

## Caveats

- Undated brochure; no test method, standard (e.g. AS/NZS 4859.1) or test conditions are cited for the U-value/R-value — treat the figure as a manufacturer claim and confirm with Ezi-Roll if used in a formal energy assessment.
- Marketed for commercial/industrial use; suitability and ratings for residential garage applications are not addressed.
