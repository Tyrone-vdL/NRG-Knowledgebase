# Ametalin SilverWrap xR Micro-perforated HD Reflective Wall Wrap — Technical Data Sheet (APM-23247-5)

## Summary

Manufacturer's technical data sheet for Ametalin SilverWrap xR Micro-perforated HD (product code HD-XR-B), a Heavy Duty, Class 4 vapour permeable, double-sided reflective wall wrap for brick veneer and drained cavity wall systems, delivering Total System R-Values of RT 1.72 winter / RT 1.68 summer (brick veneer wall, wrap only) and RT 3.40 winter / RT 3.26 summer (with an added R2.7 fibrous batt). Micro-perforations give Class 4 vapour permeance for interstitial condensation management; the wrap is an Air Barrier, IR Reflective, low flammability and suitable for all BALs. Marketed as ideal for drained cavity systems in climate zones 6–8.

## Key claims

- Total System R-Value, **brick veneer wall with SilverWrap xR Micro-perforated**: **Winter RT 1.72; Summer RT 1.68**.
- Total System R-Value, **brick veneer wall with SilverWrap xR Micro-perforated + R2.7 fibrous batt**: **Winter RT 3.40; Summer RT 3.26**.
- R-values apply to typical conditions for mainland Australian capital cities, calculated by an independent consulting engineer to **AS/NZS 4859.2:2018**.
- The outward-facing reflective anti-glare foil creates a reflective air cavity between stud frame and brick leaf, adding to system R-value without changing common construction practice.
- Vapour control: **Class 4 Vapour Permeable**; vapour permeance **1.450 µg/N·s**; vapour resistance **0.689 MN·s/g**; WVTR **176.4 g/m²·24 hr** (tested to AS/NZS 4200.1:2017).
- Air control: **Air Barrier** (AS/NZS 4200.1:2017). Water control: **Non-Water Barrier** (AS/NZS 4201.4:1994; classification involves testing under a 100 mm water column).
- Emittance value: **anti-glare side 0.05, foil side 0.03**; classification IR Reflective / IR Reflective, category RR.
- Duty: **Heavy** (AS/NZS 4200.1:2017). Flammability Index **Low (≤ 5)** (AS 1530.2-1993). Electrically conductive.
- Bushfire: complies with **AS 3959-2018** for use in **all BALs** (seek independent advice on sarking selection for the BAL design).
- Marketed as ideal for drained cavity systems in **climate zones 6–8**; the datasheet states **Class 4 vapour permeable wall wraps are not recommended for wet tropical climate zones**.
- Tensile strength 15.3 kN/m MD (min 12.5) / 13.1 kN/m LD (min 7.5); edge tear 499 N MD / 486 N LD (min 80 N); shrinkage 0.0%; products are pre-shrunk during manufacture.
- Dimensions: 1350 mm x 30 m (40 m²); nominal thickness 0.17 mm (material properties table lists 0.12 mm). Install per AS 4200.2:2017.
- NCC compliance claim: complies with AS/NZS 4859.1:2018 and AS/NZS 4200.1:2017, therefore meets NCC requirements for insulation and sarking-type materials. A detailed hygrothermal analysis is recommended for moisture control in building design.
