# ABCB Climate Zone Map — Queensland (Zones 1-8)

## Summary

ABCB state climate zone map for Queensland showing the NCC climate zones (legend Zones 1-8) against Local Government Area boundaries, including an inset for Brisbane Surrounds and a key to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Local Government Bodies. Last amended August 2015, version VC00031.3, developed from a Bureau of Meteorology map.

## Key claims

- Legend lists Zone 1 through Zone 8 mapped against Queensland Local Government Area boundaries; version VC00031.3, last amended August 2015, published by the ABCB (www.abcb.gov.au), developed from a Bureau of Meteorology map.
- Includes a "Brisbane Surrounds" inset covering south-east Queensland LGAs: Moreton Bay, Fraser Coast, Somerset, Gympie, South Burnett, Sunshine Coast, Noosa, Logan, Brisbane, Ipswich, Scenic Rim, Southern Downs, Lockyer Valley, Toowoomba, Redland and Gold Coast.
- Labels Queensland LGAs statewide, including Cairns, Townsville, Mackay, Rockhampton, Gladstone, Bundaberg, Whitsunday, Isaac, Burdekin, Hinchinbrook, Cassowary Coast, Charters Towers, Mount Isa, Longreach, Tablelands, Cook, Torres, Torres Strait Island, Northern Peninsula Area, Mareeba, Douglas and Livingstone, plus western LGAs (Bulloo, Paroo, Murweh, Quilpie, Barcoo, Diamantina, Boulia, Winton etc.).
- Includes a numbered key of 32 Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Local Government Bodies (e.g. Badu Is., Cherbourg, Doomadgee, Hopevale, Kowanyama, Lockhart River, Mapoon, Palm Island, Pormpuraaw, Saibai Is., Woorabinda, Wujal Wujal, Yarrabah).
- Town labels include Brisbane, Toowoomba, Maroochydore, Nambour, Gympie, Warwick, Maryborough, Kingaroy, Ipswich, Southport, Weipa, Cooktown, Innisfail, Bowen, Goondiwindi, St George, Roma, Charleville, Birdsville, Burketown and others.
- The PDF contains no zone-to-LGA table — zone assignments are conveyed visually by the colour-coded map only.

## Caveats

- This is a map: text extraction captures only LGA/town names and legend labels — which zone each LGA falls in is visual content not present in the text. Verify any location's climate zone against the original PDF (Z - NCC/_CLIMATE ZONE MAPS/ClimateZoneMapQLD.pdf) or the ABCB website.
- Version last amended August 2015; the ABCB has since reissued state climate zone maps (Sep 2019 releases include accessible zone tables) — check abcb.gov.au for the current version.
