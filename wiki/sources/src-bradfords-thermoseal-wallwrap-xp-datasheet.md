# Bradford Thermoseal Wall Wrap XP — Single-Sided Antiglare Wall Wrap Datasheet

## Summary

Technical datasheet for Bradford Thermoseal Wall Wrap XP, a Medium Duty, single-sided antiglare aluminium foil/polyweave wall wrap for residential brick veneer walls, classified as a Class 1 Vapour Barrier and Water Barrier under AS/NZS 4200.1. Gives emissivity values (semi-reflective 0.09 inward / non-reflective 0.90 outward), a brick veneer Total R-value of R<sub>T</sub> 1.1 summer / 1.2 winter, and the air-gap assumptions behind those figures.

## Key claims

- Medium Duty antiglare aluminium foil and polyweave fabric weather barrier, laminated with fire retardant hot melt adhesive, with a polymer coating; meets AS/NZS 4200.1; nominal thickness < 1.0 mm.
- Orientation: semi-reflective aluminium side faces outward toward the brick cavity; non-reflective polymeric side faces inward toward the stud cavity.
- Vapour Classification (ASTM E96): Class 1 Vapour Barrier; vapour permeability < 0.0022 μg/N.s. Water Control Classification (AS/NZS 4201.4): Water Barrier.
- Emissivity (AS 4201.5): inward facing semi-reflective 0.09; outward facing non-reflective 0.90. (R-value assumptions section states reflective surface ≤ 0.05 and non-reflective ≥ 0.90 — the two sections differ; verify against source.)
- Application table, brick veneer wall: Total R-value R<sub>T</sub> 1.1 summer, R<sub>T</sub> 1.2 winter.
- R-value assumptions: calculated per AS/NZS 4859.1; minimum 40 mm brick cavity air gap and 90 mm stud cavity air gap required to contribute to thermal performance; adding bulk insulation to the stud cavity diminishes the wrap's reflective air-gap R-value contribution; ventilated cavities reduce the Total R-value.
- Calculations based on a temperature difference of 6°C for heat flow out and 12°C for heat flow in.
- Flammability Index (AS 1530.2) ≤ 5 (Low); electrically Conductive (AS/NZS 3100) — keep clear of electrical wiring; tensile strength (AS/NZS 1301.448s) ≥ 9.5 machine / ≥ 7.5 lateral; edge tear (TAPPI T470) ≥ 90 both directions; shrinkage ≤ 0.5%; passes wet/dry delamination.
- Suitable in BAL regions 12.5 to FZ in accordance with AS 3959.
- Climate guidance: recommended for tropical to cool climate zones with lower wall cavity insulation levels; as a vapour barrier on the cold side of the construction it may increase condensation entrapment risk — in cooler climate zones consider the Enviroseal vapour permeable range instead.
- Not recommended behind lightweight cladding except in tropical climate zone 1 (check with the cladding manufacturer).
- Roll sizes: 1350 mm x 30 m (40.5 m²), 1350 mm x 60 m (81 m²), 1500 mm x 30 m (45 m²), 1500 mm x 60 m (90 m²).
- Do not crease, crush or sharply fold during installation (maintains the water barrier property); install per AS 4200.2.

## Caveats

- Revision Level A; no publication date in extracted text — check bradfordinsulation.com.au for the latest version.
- Internal inconsistency in the extract: classification table lists inward-facing emissivity 0.09, while the R-value assumptions paragraph cites reflective surface emittance ≤ 0.05 — verify against the source PDF.
- The R<sub>T</sub> 1.1/1.2 values are for the depicted brick veneer application with the stated empty air gaps; they are not a material R-value and do not apply once the stud cavity is insulated.
