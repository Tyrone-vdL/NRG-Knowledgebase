# Greenboard Wall Cladding Thermal Rating (Total R-value Build-ups)

## Summary

A one-page thermal rating sheet for Greenboard EPS wall cladding (NRG Greenboard) on timber or steel frame, tabulating the total wall system R-value layer by layer for 40/60/75/100 mm board thicknesses — total R-values from R1.92 (40 mm) to R3.48 (100 mm). Useful for NatHERS wall construction inputs because it shows both the material R-value of the board and the full rendered-wall build-up.

## Key claims

- Greenboard material R-values by thickness: 40 mm = R1.04, 60 mm = R1.56, 75 mm = R1.95, 100 mm = R2.60.
- Total wall system R-values (rendered Greenboard on timber or steel frame): 40 mm = R1.92, 60 mm = R2.44, 75 mm = R2.83, 100 mm = R3.48.
- The build-up layers and their R contributions (constant across thicknesses): external air film R0.03, render system R0.02, breather sisalation wrap R0.48, cavity/frame R0.17, 10 mm plasterboard R0.06, internal air film R0.12.
- The R0.48 attributed to the "breather sisalation wrap" implies a reflective airspace contribution, not the membrane material alone.
- Defines R-value as the measure of a material's resistance to heat transfer — the higher the R-value, the greater the insulating effect; states total R-value ratings "where checked by" a third party (name appears only as a logo, not in extractable text).
- Supplier contacts on the sheet: NRG Greenboard (sales@nrggreenboard.com, www.nrggreenboard.com, West Burleigh QLD) and qbears.com.au (Springwood QLD, ph 1300 304 313).

## Concepts touched

walls, wall cladding, EPS, Greenboard, R-value, total R-value, wraps, render systems, NatHERS, 7-Star

## Caveats

- Undated, supplier-issued sheet; the verifying body is shown only as a logo and is not identifiable from the extracted text — no AS/NZS 4859.1 test reference is quoted.
- Total R-values assume the exact build-up listed (rendered board, wrap with cavity, 10 mm plasterboard); different cavities or membranes change the total. The cavity/frame R0.17 does not model thermal bridging through studs explicitly.
- Table layout degraded in extraction; verify figures against the source PDF.
