# NCC Volume Two Energy Efficiency — Group Exercise Handout (ABCB Resource Kit)

## Summary

ABCB NCC Resource Kit training handout (2016) containing two group exercises on the NCC Volume Two energy efficiency DtS Provisions: Exercise 1 on minimum Total R-Values (definitions, climate-zone lookups, insulation penetration adjustments) and Exercise 2 on glazing compliance under 3.12.2.1(a). It is a question sheet for trainers — it poses worked-example scenarios but does not supply the answers.

## Key claims

- Exercise 1 tests understanding of: R-Value vs Total R-Value, which house elements and clauses carry minimum Total R-Values, and zone-specific lookups (e.g. minimum Total R-Value for a roof/ceiling in climate zone 1; a roof with flat ceiling in climate zone 2 below 300 m altitude with roof solar absorptance 0.55).
- It asks for inherent Total R-Values of un-insulated pitched metal roofs with flat ceilings (climate zone 2 below 300 m altitude, and climate zone 6) and added-insulation requirements for pitched dark tiled and metal roofs — answers must be looked up in NCC Volume Two Part 3.12.1.
- Worked-example scenario parameters: building in climate zone 3; floor area 100 m²; heat flow downward and upward; red metal roof with solar absorptance value 0.75; unventilated roof space; one 1 m² service opening in the insulation; 11 light openings of 100 mm diameter.
- The worked example asks students to calculate the uninsulated roof R-Value, the percentage of ceiling insulation lost to the service opening and lights, and the adjusted minimum ceiling insulation R-Value to compensate — i.e. the insulation penetration compensation method in the DtS Provisions.
- Exercise 2 covers glazing: definitions of external glazing, Total System U-Value and Total System SHGC, and a compliance check under clause 3.12.2.1(a).
- Exercise 2 scenario: house in climate zone 5; single storey slab-on-ground; 100 m² rectangular plan with long axis east-west; 300 mm eaves on all sides; window head 100 mm below eaves; standard ventilation; single glazed clear aluminium windows with Total System U-Value 7.9 W/m².K and Total System SHGC 0.81.

## Concepts touched

NCC, energy efficiency, training, insulation, R-value, Total R-Value, solar absorptance, climate zones, glazing, U-value, SHGC, roofing, ceiling penetrations

## Caveats

- Training exercise sheet only — contains questions, not authoritative answers; values must be derived from NCC Volume Two Part 3.12 (2016-era clause numbering, 6-star stringency, superseded by NCC 2022 Part H6 / Housing Provisions at 7-star).
- The glazing schedule and house plan for Exercise 2 are drawings not captured in text extraction; refer to the source PDF.
