# NCC Volume Two Energy Efficiency Resource Kit — Read Me (ABCB)

## Summary

One-page Read Me (2016) introducing the ABCB's NCC Resource Kit on the Energy Efficiency Provisions in Volume Two of the NCC — an educational package for TAFE/university teachers, industry association trainers and employers. It explains how the kit's components (FAQs, case studies, group exercises and a PowerPoint presentation) can be tailored to different audiences.

## Key claims

- The kit provides educational resources for an introduction and overview of the Energy Efficiency Provisions in NCC Volume Two.
- Components include FAQs, Case Studies and Group Exercises, plus a main PowerPoint presentation with a table of contents in the first few slides.
- It can be delivered standalone, but the ABCB recommends having access to the NCC (online or hardcopy) while working through it; the NCC is free online via registration at www.abcb.gov.au.
- Trainers familiar with the NCC may use PowerPoint's "hide" function to present only relevant slides; teaching professionals may present the full deck.
- Published 2016 under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International Licence by the Commonwealth and States and Territories of Australia.

## Concepts touched

NCC, energy efficiency, training, NCC Resource Kit, NCC Volume Two

## Caveats

- 2016 kit covering the NCC 2016-era Volume Two energy efficiency provisions (6-star, Part 3.12) — superseded by NCC 2022 Part H6 / 7-star / Whole-of-Home; useful as training-structure context only.
- Navigation/introduction page with no technical content; the substantive material lives in the companion FAQ, case study and exercise documents.
