# VELUX Skylights and Roof Windows — Product Information and Price List 2018

## Summary

VELUX Australia's 2018 product information and price list covering pitched-roof skylights (VS, VSS, VSE, FS), flat-roof skylights (VCS, VCM, FCM), roof windows (GGL, GPL) and Sun Tunnels (TWF, TWR), with U-values, SHGC, WERS ratings and bushfire (BAL) performance for each product line. Useful for NatHERS assessments because it gives whole-of-skylight thermal performance figures (not just glass-only) and notes inclusion in AccuRate, BASIX and FirstRate rating programmes. Includes glazing variant details (High Performance "2004" vs Comfort "2005" double glazing) and RRP pricing effective February 2018.

## Key claims

- VELUX skylights and roof windows are included in the national energy rating programme (AccuRate) and state programmes BASIX and FirstRate.
- High Performance double glazing (code 2004): laminated inner pane, toughened outer pane, double Low-E³ coating, argon-filled cavity, warm edge technology; approx 75% radiant heat block, approx 99% UV block, 32 dB noise reduction, WERS 5 stars (summer rating).
- Comfort double glazing (code 2005): toughened safety glass, Low-E³ coating, argon fill; approx 95% UV block, 29 dB noise reduction, WERS 5 stars (summer rating).
- Pitched-roof opening skylights VS/VSS: U-value 2.50 W/m²K (complete skylight) / 1.93 (glass only); SHGC 0.21 (skylight) / 0.28 (glass only). Fixed FS: U-value 2.60 (skylight), SHGC 0.24.
- Flat-roof skylights VCS/VCM: U-value 2.9 W/m²K (skylight) / 1.93 (glass only); SHGC 0.24. Fixed FCM: U-value 2.9, SHGC 0.28. The 2.9 U-value is only achieved when installed on a timber hob/base with plasterboard (Gyprock) as per the typical installation detail.
- U-value and SHGC figures are based on AFRC simulations for 2004 glazing at standard sizes (VS/VSS/VSE 1140 x 1180 mm; VCS/VCM/FCM 1275 x 1275 mm).
- Roof windows GGL (centre-pivot) and GPL (dual action): U-value 1.30 W/m²K (window) / 1.00 (glass only); SHGC 0.31 (window) / 0.30 (glass); calculated to EN 14351-1:2006 +A2:2016 at 1140 x 1400 mm; laminated double glazing standard.
- Bushfire: skylights with 2004 glazing assessed to AS 3959 at BAL 40 (tested closed, custom flashing required); rigid Sun Tunnel (TWR) rated BAL 29.
- Under AS 1288, skylights installed 3 m or more above floor level must use laminated glass (the 2004 High Performance glazing variant).
- All VELUX skylights/roof windows except Sun Tunnels are deemed non-combustible per a CSIRO assessment, complying with BCA fire separation requirements (suitable within 900 mm of a boundary for Class 1 and 10 buildings).
- Minimum roof pitch: pitched-roof skylights and roof windows require 15° (below that they must be raised to 15° and custom flashed); flat-roof products suit 0°–60° with a 3°–5° pitch recommended to avoid ponding; Sun Tunnels are not suitable below 15°.
- Blockout blinds reduce light by approx 100% and heat by approx 40%; honeycomb blinds reduce light by approx 95% and heat by approx 60% (VELUX internal testing, 3076 model roof window).
- Sun Tunnels: 350 mm diameter, dual-layered ceiling diffuser spreads light over up to 9 m²; flexible (TWF, 2200 mm) and rigid (TWR, 1650 mm) shafts.
- Warranty: 10 years on skylights, roof windows, flashings and insulated glass seal; 3 years on accessories including electric controls and blinds.
- Price list effective February 2018 (RRP, supply only, GST inclusive) — e.g. FS fixed skylight from $397, VS manual from $826, VSS solar from $2,053, GGL roof window from $690, TWF Sun Tunnel $330.

## Caveats

- 2018 brochure with prices effective February 2018 — product range, specifications and pricing are dated and likely superseded; verify current VELUX data before quoting.
- Bushfire, cyclone and overhead-glazing compliance carry footnoted conditions (closed position, custom flashing, 2004 glazing variant only); check the source PDF footnotes before relying on a specific rating.
- References BCA 2013 fall-prevention provisions — predates NCC 2019/2022 terminology.
