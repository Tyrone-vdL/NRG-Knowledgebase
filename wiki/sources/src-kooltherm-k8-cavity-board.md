# Kingspan Kooltherm K8 Cavity Board — Cavity Wall Insulation Datasheet

## Summary

Kingspan datasheet (Issue 14, October 2018) for Kooltherm K8 Cavity Board, a rigid thermoset phenolic insulation board with low-emissivity foil facings for masonry cavity walls, with thermal conductivity 0.021 W/m·K and product R-values of R1.2 (25 mm) and R1.9 (40 mm). Provides Total R-values for double-brick and brick/block cavity walls and installation requirements including retention of a 40 mm residual clear cavity.

## Key claims

- Thermal conductivity (λ-value): 0.021 W/m·K for insulant thickness 25–44 mm.
- Product R-values: 25 mm = R1.2; 40 mm = R1.9.
- Total R-values, double brick cavity wall (with 40 mm airspace): 25 mm board RT2.3 heat flow in / RT2.5 heat flow out; 40 mm board RT3.1 in / RT3.2 out.
- Total R-values, brick/block cavity wall (190 mm corefilled block inner leaf, 40 mm airspace): 25 mm board RT2.3 in / RT2.4 out; 40 mm board RT3.0 in / RT3.2 out. Stated as Total R-values for the building element per the BCA energy provisions; contribution depends on installation and environmental conditions.
- Foil face emittance: E0.06; the low-emissivity facings increase the thermal resistance of the adjacent cavity.
- Board size 1200 mm × 514 mm (0.61 m²); thicknesses 25 and 40 mm standard (others on enquiry).
- Compressive stress (AS 2498.3): typically exceeds 100 kPa at 10% compression; water vapour resistance (BS EN 12086) > 100 MN·s/g.
- Fire (AS 1530.3): Spread of Flame Index 0, Smoke Development ≤ 3.
- Compliant with AS/NZS 4859.1; CodeMark-certified for NCC compliance (Certificate CM20042); made in Australia.
- Installation: secure to external face of the inner leaf with double-drip wall ties and retaining discs/clips; maintain a residual cavity of at least 40 mm per the moisture penetration provisions of AS 3700 as called up in NCC Volume One B1.4; install per AS 3999:2015 Section 4 safety requirements and AS 3000 wiring rules (foil facings conduct electricity).
- Zero ODP, low-GWP CFC/HCFC-free blowing agent; 10-year warranty for residential and commercial installations; boards that have been wet, damaged or contacted MEK-based solvent adhesives must not be used.

## Caveats

- Issue 14 dated October 2018; NCC clause reference (Volume One B1.4) is pre-NCC 2022 numbering — verify the current datasheet and certificate before specifying.
- Quoted Total R-values assume the specific wall constructions and 40 mm airspace shown; different leaf materials or cavity widths change the result.
