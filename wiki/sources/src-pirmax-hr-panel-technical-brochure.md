# PIRmax HR Panel Technical Brochure (PIR Rigid Insulation)

## Summary

Technical brochure (v2.5) for PIRmax HR Panel, an Australian-made modified polyisocyanurate (PIR) rigid insulation panel with double-sided aluminium foil facings, giving high R-values per millimetre (R1.0 at 20 mm up to R5.0 at 100 mm) where cavity space is minimal. Covers thermal data, the full thickness/R-value table, fire classifications, panel sizes and soffit fixing instructions.

## Key claims

- Thermal conductivity: 0.020 W/mK; foil facings have reflectivity 94% and emissivity 0.06 (silver foil face).
- Product R-values by thickness: 20 mm = R1.0, 25 mm = R1.25, 30 mm = R1.5, 40 mm = R2.0, 50 mm = R2.5, 60 mm = R3.0, 70 mm = R3.5, 75 mm = R3.8, 80 mm = R4.0, 90 mm = R4.5, 100 mm = R5.0.
- Complies with AS/NZS 4859.1; described as NCC/BCA compliant.
- Achieves Group 1 BCA/NCC fire classification when tested to AS ISO 9705:2003 (compliant to the 2016 NCC); also Group 1 lining to AS/NZS 3837:1998.
- AS 1530.3 regulatory indices: Ignitability 0 (range 0-20), Spread of Flame 0 (range 0-10), Heat Evolved 0 (range 0-10), Smoke Developed 1 (range 0-10).
- Compressive strength > 125 kPa; water vapour resistivity > 100 MNs/gm; square edge treatment.
- Standard panel size 2400 × 1200 mm (2.88 m²); WA sizes 2270 × 1200 mm and 514 × 2270 mm; custom cuts available; facings in silver/white/black embossed aluminium both sides.
- Applications: soffits (carpark), external/internal walls, raised underfloor, ground floor slabs, ceilings, roofs, ducting, external cladding.
- CFC free, zero ODP, zero VOCs, not corrosive to metal, closed-cell foam core, manufactured in Australia (PIRMAX Pty Ltd, Braeside VIC).
- Soffit fixing: 11 insulation fasteners recommended per board (5 acceptable for suspended ceilings / no wind pressure), minimum 35 mm fastener head diameter, joints taped with minimum 75 mm wide aluminium foil tape, minimum 25 mm clearance to fire collar penetrations.

## Caveats

- Brochure version 2.5; fire-classification claim references the 2016 NCC — verify Group 1 status against current NCC editions (2019/2022).
- R-values stated as product (material) R-values, not system/total R-values; added reflective airspace benefit depends on installation.
- Brochure carries a 2016 copyright (Reflex Insulation Pty Ltd) and a manufacturer disclaimer; confirm current specifications with PIRMAX.
