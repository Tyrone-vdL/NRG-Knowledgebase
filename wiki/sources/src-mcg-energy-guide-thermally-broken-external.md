# MCG Windows & Doors Thermally Broken WERS Energy Guide

## Summary

A one-page WERS energy guide from M.C.G Windows & Doors listing U-values and SHGC values for their thermally broken (TB) aluminium window and door range, all with double glazed low-E glass. It covers the Signature TB and Essential TB product lines (hinged doors, sliding doors, sliding windows, fixed windows, XFL fixed windows and awnings) with WERS product codes (BRD-xxx-xx format) for use in NatHERS glazing inputs.

## Key claims

- All products listed are thermally broken aluminium frames with double glazed low-E glazing.
- Product ranges covered: Signature TB Hinged Doors, TB Sliding Doors, Signature TB Sliding Windows, Essential TB Fixed Windows, Signature TB XFL Fixed Windows, Essential TB Awnings, Signature TB Awnings.
- WERS IDs listed include BRD-109-41, BRD-104-20, BRD-151-40, BRD-097-55, BRD-101-53, BRD-102-41 and BRD-150-40.
- U-values across the listed TB range span 1.9 to 2.6 (W/m²K, WERS whole-of-window values).
- SHGC values across the listed TB range span 0.42 to 0.52.
- The one unambiguous code-to-value pairing in the extraction: BRD-150-40, double glazed low-E, U-value 2.3 with an adjacent fixed-window entry showing U-value 1.9 and SHGC 0.52 (pairing of these two rows is degraded — verify against the PDF).
- Contact: M.C.G Windows & Doors, ph 1300 766 349, mcgwindows.com.au.

## Caveats

- Table layout degraded in extraction — the code-to-U-value-to-SHGC row alignment is scrambled; verify individual product figures against the source PDF before quoting in an assessment.
- Undated single-page guide; WERS ratings can be revised — confirm current values on the WERS database before certification use.
- A companion guide for MCG's standard (non-thermally-broken) range exists in the same library ("MCG Energy Guide - External.pdf").
