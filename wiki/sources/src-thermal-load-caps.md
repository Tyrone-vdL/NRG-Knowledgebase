# BASIX Thermal Load Caps by NatHERS Climate Zone (Heating/Cooling, Slab vs Suspended Floor)

## Summary

A table of BASIX (NSW) thermal load caps in MJ/m².yr by NatHERS climate zone, giving the total load cap plus separate heating and cooling caps for slab-on-ground and suspended-floor construction — the limits a NSW dwelling's simulated loads must not exceed under BASIX thermal comfort (simulation method). Eighteen NSW climate zones are visible (zones 8 through 69), with zones 25 and 69 having "No Max" on individual heating/cooling caps.
