# Kingspan AIR-CELL Glareshield Thermo-Reflective Anti-Glare Insulation

## Summary

Kingspan datasheet (Issue 4, April 2011) for AIR-CELL Glareshield, a 7 mm thermo-reflective anti-glare insulation roll combining insulation, vapour barrier and radiant barrier in one product, with a material R-value of R0.14 and system Total R-values up to RT3.1 depending on application and heat-flow direction. Relevant to roof and wall reflective-insulation inputs where the system R-value depends on adjacent air spaces and installation.

## Key claims

- Material R-value: R0.14 (thermal resistance tested to ASTM C518); product thickness 7 mm.
- Emittance (ASTM E408): reflective face E0.03, anti-glare face E0.05; reflectance: reflective face 97%, anti-glare face 95%.
- Total R-values for building elements (as required by the BCA energy provisions), heat flow in / heat flow out: residential metal roof (ventilated) RT2.6 / RT1.2; residential brick veneer wall RT1.8 / RT2.1; commercial warehouse roof RT1.9 / RT0.9; commercial office roof RT3.1 / RT1.4.
- The datasheet warns Total R-value contribution depends on installation and environmental conditions, and the R-value will be reduced by dust accumulation on upward-facing surfaces and in ventilated cavities.
- Manufactured, tested and packaged in conformance with AS/NZS 4859.1:2002 Amdt 1; CodeMark-certified for BCA compliance (certificate SAIG-09-CM20001).
- Marketed as helping achieve a 6-star house energy rating (pre-7-Star era document).
- Roll: 1350 mm × 22.25 m (30 m²), 440 mm roll diameter, 11 kg; duty rating Extra Heavy (AS 4200.1 6.1.4); maximum span 2.4 m without support mesh.
- Fire/material testing: AS 1530.2 flammability Low (Index ≤ 5); AS 1530.3 result 0/0/0/0-1; AS/NZS 3837 cone calorimeter Group 1; corrosion resistance AS/NZS 4859.1 Appendix 1 Pass.
- Membrane testing: vapour transmission ASTM E96 Medium; water penetration AS/NZS 4201.4 High Resistance; dry/wet delamination AS/NZS 4201.1/.2 Pass; shrinkage AS/NZS 4201.3 ≤ 0.5%; edge tear AS 4200.1 6.1.3 lateral 551 N / machine 514 N; burst force AS 3706.4 1400 N.
- Acoustics: 6 dB(A) noise reduction (Bassett Acoustics report MA0221j02).
- Fibre-free, non-allergenic, water-resistant, anti-bacterial/anti-fungal, rodent and insect resistant.

## Caveats

- Issue 4 dated April 2011, referencing 6-star compliance and AS/NZS 4859.1:2002 — predates NCC 2022 / 7-Star and AS/NZS 4859.1:2018; confirm the product's current datasheet and certification before use.
- Quoted Total R-values are system values for specific constructions with air spaces, not product R-values — they collapse if the cavity geometry differs or surfaces get dusty.
