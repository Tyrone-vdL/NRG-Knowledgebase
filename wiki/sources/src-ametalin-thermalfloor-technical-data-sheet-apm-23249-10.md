# Ametalin ThermalFloor 2-in-1 Reflective Underfloor Insulation — Technical Data Sheet (APM-23249-10)

## Summary

Manufacturer's technical data sheet for Ametalin ThermalFloor (product code TF), a 4 mm XPE foam-core, double-sided reflective foil underfloor insulation and moisture-management system for floors adhered directly to joists, giving Total System R-Values of RT 3.47 winter / RT 1.63 summer (enclosed unventilated sub-floor) and RT 2.16 winter / RT 0.90 summer (enclosed ventilated sub-floor). The product has a material R-value of R0.1 and is classified Extra Heavy duty under AS/NZS 4200.1:2017. Scored for quick installation, with weep holes for drainage.

## Key claims

- Total System R-Value, **enclosed sub-floor, unventilated**, with ThermalFloor: **Winter RT 3.47; Summer RT 1.63**.
- Total System R-Value, **enclosed sub-floor, ventilated**, with ThermalFloor: **Winter RT 2.16; Summer RT 0.90**.
- **Material R-value: R0.1** (0.1 m²·K/W, ASTM C518) — the system R-values above are achieved when incorporated into typical construction systems.
- R-values apply to typical conditions for mainland Australian capital cities, calculated by an independent consulting engineer in accordance with **AS/NZS 4859.2:2018**; contribution depends on installation and environmental conditions.
- Construction: aluminium foil laminates, **97% reflectivity, emissivity 0.03 to both sides**, around a 4 mm chemically cross-linked closed-cell high-density XPE foam core.
- Emittance value: 0.03 printed side, 0.03 foil side; Emittance classification: IR Reflective, IR Reflective; category RR.
- Vapour control: Class 3 Vapour Permeable; vapour permeance **0.0143 µg/N·s**. Water control: **Non-Water Barrier**. Air control: Non-Air Barrier.
- Duty: **Extra Heavy** per AS/NZS 4200.1:2017. Nominal thickness **4 mm**. Flammability Index **Low (≤ 5)** (AS 1530.2-1993). Electrically conductive.
- Tensile strength 15.6 kN/m MD (min 13.0) / 14.8 kN/m LD (min 10.5); edge tear 853 N MD / 804 N LD (min 90 N). Shrinkage 0.0%.
- NCC compliance claim: complies with AS/NZS 4859.1:2018 and AS/NZS 4200.1:2017, therefore meets NCC requirements for insulation and sarking-type materials.
- Designed for use under floors with a sealed air space between flooring and enclosed sub-floor space; weep holes allow water and water vapour to permeate through.
- Roll dimensions: 500 mm x 60 m (30 m²). Install per AS 4200.2:2017.
