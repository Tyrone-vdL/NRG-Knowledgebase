# Solaris Insulated Roof Panel Construction & Applications Guide

## Summary

Technical guide (Retracom, last updated 09/05/2011) for the Solaris insulated roof panel — a long-spanning all-in-one EPS-core sandwich panel with COLORBOND steel skins, in thicknesses 50-150 mm giving built-up roof system R-values of R1.7 to R4.3 (m²K/W). Covers specification, span/wind-pressure tables, bearing capacities, domestic awning spans and roof drainage limits; flagged as Part J compliant for commercial/industrial energy efficiency.

## Key claims

- R-values (m²K/W, "built up systems: roof"): 50 mm = 1.7, 75 mm = 2.4, 100 mm = 3.0, 125 mm = 3.7, 150 mm = 4.3.
- Panel weights: 50 mm = 10 kg/m², 75 mm = 10.3, 100 mm = 10.7, 125 mm = 11.0, 150 mm = 11.3 kg/m².
- Construction: SL grade expanded polystyrene (EPS) core with void-filled profile, fire-retardant treated; roof skin 0.42 mm hi-tensile pre-painted COLORBOND steel; ceiling skin 0.5 mm pre-painted COLORBOND steel; module width 1000 mm; trapezoidal 36 mm rib profile; tongue-and-groove Retracom Flushline MkII joints.
- Lengths 2400 mm to 16,000 mm; minimum pitch 1.5° single span / 5° multi span; maximum skin temperature 80°C; expansion joints required at no more than 16 m spacing.
- BCA Materials Group Classification: Group 2 (Group 1 available with additional fixings); marketed as Part J compliant for air-conditioned commercial/industrial buildings.
- Spans up to 9700 mm for 150 mm panel at 0.5 kPa ultimate design wind pressure; full allowable-span tables given by panel thickness (50-150 mm) for pressures 0.5-8 kPa, based on 1:500-year ultimate wind, deflection limits span/150 (1:20-year wind) and span/200 with 0.25 kPa/1.1 kN live load to AS 1170.
- Thermal bowing of up to span/180 may develop for a 35°C temperature gradient (-10°C to 60°C external, 25°C internal); thermal effects not included in span tables.
- Fixing: 14g tek screws with washers to each rib (printed "14kg" in extraction); pullout capacity to be checked.
- Domestic awning span tables provided for wind ratings N1-N5 and C1-C4 (75 mm only for cyclonic) across 3-sides-open through enclosed cases, e.g. N1/150 mm/3-sides-open Case A spans 9400 mm with 900 mm overhang.
- Maximum roof length limited by drainage: e.g. at 150 mm/hr rainfall intensity, 16 m at 1.5-3° slopes (continuous sheet ridge to gutter) up to 290 m at 10° slope; 15-year delamination warranty from Retracom.

## Caveats

- Last updated 09/05/2011 — "Part J compliant" and BCA Group classifications predate NCC 2019/2022; re-verify fire (group classification for EPS-core panels) and energy claims against the current NCC before specifying.
- R-values are quoted for "built up systems: roof" without stated test conditions or AS/NZS 4859.1 reference in this document.
- Span, wind and bearing tables had degraded layout in extraction; verify any figure against the source PDF before structural use.
