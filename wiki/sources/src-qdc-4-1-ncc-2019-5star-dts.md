# QDC MP 4.1 Sustainable Buildings — Version 1.13 (17 Sep 2020, commenced 1 Mar 2021)

## Summary

Full text of Queensland Development Code MP 4.1 Sustainable Buildings Version 1.13 (published 17 September 2020, commenced 1 March 2021), the mandatory Queensland overlay that varied the BCA energy efficiency provisions for Class 1 and Class 2 buildings — setting a 6-star software rating default for houses with nominal-credit pathways down to 4.5 stars, Schedule 5 heating/cooling load limits for NatHERS climate zones 7, 9, 10, 19 and 50, plus water efficiency (WELS), electricity sub-metering and end-of-trip facility requirements. Despite the library filename "NCC 2019 5STAR DTS", the document is the QDC MP 4.1 code itself; its acceptable solutions reference BCA 2009/2010 elemental provisions and BCA 2019 V2.6.2.2 verification.

## Key claims

- **A1 (Class 1 thermal performance)** — a house complies via any of: (2) BCA 2010 Vol 2 parts 3.12.1–3.12.4 (in climate zones 1 and 2, disregarding 3.12.1.5(a)(i) and (iii)); (3) reference building verification per **V2.6.2.2 of BCA 2019**; (4) **software rating ≥ 6 stars without nominal credit** (plus Schedule 5 load limits where in NatHERS CZ 7, 9, 10, 19 or 50); (5) BCA CZ 1, 2 or 5: **≥ 4.5 stars + nominal credit to total 6 stars**; (6) BCA CZ 3: **≥ 5 stars + nominal credit to total 6 stars**; (7) BCA 2009 parts 3.12.1–3.12.4 + up to 1 star nominal credit.
- **A1(8) nominal credits**: (a) **0.5 star** — outdoor living area with roof covering achieving **Total R-Value ≥ 1.5 (downward heat flow)**; (b) **1 star** — same roof + **permanently installed ceiling fan with speed controller and blade rotation diameter ≥ 900 mm**; (c) **1 star** — **solar PV system ≥ 1 kW** capacity (maximum power output).
- **Outdoor living area definition (s11)**: directly accessible from and attached to a living area (lounge, kitchen, dining, family); **floor area ≥ 12.0 m²; minimum dimension 2.5 m in all directions**; fully covered by impervious roof; Class 1 — two or more sides open or readily openable; Class 2 SOU — at least one side open/readily openable (excluding the internal connection).
- **A2 (Class 2 thermal performance)** — each sole-occupancy unit complies with JV1 of BCA 2009 Vol 1, modified: each SOU **≥ 4 stars** (plus Schedule 5 load limits in NatHERS CZ 7, 9, 10, 19, 50) and the **average of all SOUs ≥ 5 stars**; Section J DTS provisions have no effect for SOUs.
- **A2(2)–(3) Class 2 nominal credits (BCA CZ 1 or 2)**: 0.5 star for an OLA with roof Total R-Value 1.5 (downward); 1 star if it also has a permanently fixed ceiling fan (speed controller, blade ≥ 900 mm) — but for the 1-star credit, **an air-conditioner serving any room directly adjacent to the OLA must automatically shut down when an external door to the OLA is open for more than 1 minute**.
- **Section 4 hot water variation**: BCA performance requirement **P2.6.2 and DTS clause 3.12.5.6 do not apply** to hot water systems in Queensland for Class 1/10 buildings; A5 permits any water heater type including electric resistance.
- **Heating/cooling load limits (ss 9–10, Schedule 5)** apply only in NatHERS climate zones **7 (Rockhampton), 9 (Amberley), 10 (Brisbane), 19 (Charleville), 50 (Oakey)**, by floor type and software rating. Examples from Table A (Class 1, MJ/m².annum): CZ 10 Timber at 6 stars — heating 28, cooling 31; CZ 10 Concrete at 6 stars — heating 24, cooling 31; CZ 7 Timber at 6 stars — heating 18, cooling 86. Table B gives Class 2 SOU limits (concrete floors, 4–6 stars), e.g. CZ 10 Concrete 6 stars — heating 25, cooling 32.
- **Lighting (A3/A4)**: Class 1 — comply with BCA 2010 Part 3.12.5.5 OR provide **energy efficient lighting (≥ 27 lumens/W) for ≥ 80% of total fixed artificial lighting**; Class 2 SOUs — the 80% energy-efficient-lighting rule.
- **Water conservation (A6–A8)**, where serviced by a water service provider: shower roses **≥ 3-star WELS**; toilets **dual-flush, ≥ 4-star WELS** with cistern/bowl compatibility; tapware ≥ 3-star WELS for laundry tubs, kitchen sinks and basins.
- **Electricity sub-metering (A9–A11)**: each meterable premises (Class 2/5 SOUs) needs its own sub-meter (accuracy class 1 per AS 62053.21:2005 or AS 1284.1:2004), installed in a common area, unobstructed, and labelled.
- **End-of-trip facilities (P12/A12)** for major developments (> 2000 m² offices, shopping centres, tertiary facilities, hospitals) in designated local government areas (Schedule 1 lists 22 councils incl. Brisbane, Gold Coast, **Sunshine Coast**, Noosa, Moreton Bay): bicycle parking at 5% of workforce or Schedule 3 occupant ratios (e.g. offices 1 per 30 occupants), lockers at 1.6 per bike space (900x300x450 mm), change rooms/showers/sanitary per Schedule 4 (e.g. 20+ bike spaces → 2 showers per gender + 1 per additional 20 spaces).
- **Flood exemption (s3(2))**: MP 4.1 and relevant BCA provisions do not apply to building work that raises a flood/storm-surge affected house, where certified.
- Renovations of Class 1 buildings and Class 2 SOUs attract performance requirements P1/P2 (and P3/P4) but not the services/water/sub-metering suite applied to new builds (per Table 1 application matrix).

## Caveats

- **Version 1.13 (Sep 2020) — superseded for current work.** NCC 2022's 7-star regime and the current QDC MP 4.1 edition (see [[src-qdc-mp4-1-sustainable-buildings]]) replace these star-rating and credit settings; this page is the historical/transition reference (relevant for alterations to buildings approved under the prior regime).
- Filename in the library says "NCC 2019 5STAR DTS" but the content is the QDC MP 4.1 code; the 5-star association is via the 4.5/5-star + credit pathways, not a literal 5-star DTS document.
- Schedule 5 load-limit tables extracted from a wide PDF table — verify individual cell values against the source PDF before quoting.
