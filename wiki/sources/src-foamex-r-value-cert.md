# Foamex Thermal Insulation Evaluation — Flat Concrete Roof with 65mm Reflective EPS (R3.22 Winter)

## Summary

Engineering calculation certificate (by James Fricker, M.AIRAH, CPEng, calculated 2/7/08) determining the winter Total R-value of a flat concrete roof system insulated with 65 mm reflective SL-grade polystyrene (EPS with reflective foil laminate), a sealed 50 mm reflective cavity, and an unventilated 70–600 mm reflective cavity above a suspended ceiling. The headline result is a winter Total R-value of R3.22 m².K/W per AS/NZS 4859.1:2002/Amdt 1 2006 Clause K3.1, with an application table extending the result to other EPS grades (R3.14–R3.49).

## Key claims

- System evaluated (outside-in): outside air film R0.040; roof membrane R0.061; 150 mm concrete slab R0.104; sealed reflective 50 mm air space R0.585; 65 mm SL Grade EPS with RFL R1.647; unventilated reflective 70–600 mm air space R0.613; suspended 10 mm plasterboard ceiling R0.059; indoor air film (unreflective) R0.110.
- Winter Total R-value: R3.22 m².K/W for the system, for 12.0°C ambient / 18.0°C inside air (ΔT 6 K, 15.0°C mean); corresponding total conductance kTi = 0.31 W/(m².K).
- Determination per AS/NZS 4859.1:2002/Amdt 1 2006, Clause K3.1, incorporating the standard's dust assumptions (Clauses K3.2 & K4.2) for reflective surfaces.
- Foil emittances assumed: SL Grade EPS RFL and double-sided antiglare foil infrared emittances of 0.10 and 0.03.
- EPS conductivity basis: k = 0.0407 W/m.K at 23°C for SL grade; R adjusted 0.39%/K per Clause K3.1.
- Application table for 65 mm EPS at 15.0°C mean, ΔT 6 K — EPS R-value / Total system R / Added R: Class L (k 0.0427) 1.571 / 3.14 / 2.59; Class SL (k 0.0407) 1.647 / 3.22 / 2.67; Class S (k 0.0394) 1.702 / 3.27 / 2.72; Class M (k 0.0380) 1.764 / 3.34 / 2.78; Class H (k 0.0365) 1.836 / 3.41 / 2.85; Class VH (k 0.0349) 1.919 / 3.49 / 2.94 (k in W/m·K, R in m²·K/W).
- Cavity air-space R-values estimated using Reflect3 software (iterative calculations per USA Division of Housing Research Paper 32, ±0.05 m²·K/W); cavities over 70 mm are calculated as 70 mm because extrapolation is prohibited by AS/NZS 4859.1.
- Thermal short-circuiting by frames is explicitly not considered — the evaluation is for the insulation path only.
- The report states it may not be reproduced except in full, and results may not be quoted without reference to the assumptions.

## Caveats

- Winter (heat flow up/out) evaluation only at ΔT 6 K — no summer (heat flow down) R-value is provided, and reflective-cavity systems perform differently by heat-flow direction.
- Calculated 2/7/2008 against AS/NZS 4859.1:2002/Amdt 1 2006, since superseded by AS/NZS 4859.1:2018; emittance and dust assumptions may differ under the current standard.
- Result is specific to the exact construction described (sealed cavities, suspended ceiling, no frame bridging) — not a generic product R-value.
