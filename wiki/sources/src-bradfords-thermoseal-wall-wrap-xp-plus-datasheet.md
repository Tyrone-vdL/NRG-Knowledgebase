# Bradford Thermoseal Wall Wrap XP PLUS — Double-Sided Reflective Wall Wrap Datasheet

## Summary

Technical datasheet for Bradford Thermoseal Wall Wrap XP PLUS, a double-sided reflective foil wall wrap for brick veneer walls with a patterned aluminium antiglare outer surface (reflects up to 91% of radiant heat) and a silver foil inner surface. Gives AS/NZS 4200.1 classifications, emittance values (E0.09 antiglare / E0.05 silver), and a brick veneer Total R-value example of R<sub>T</sub> 1.6 summer / 1.9 winter from the wrap and air gaps alone.

## Key claims

- Double-sided reflective wall wrap: patterned aluminium antiglare outer surface laminated to a woven polymeric fabric and an inward-facing aluminium foil, with fire retardant adhesives.
- Antiglare surface reflects up to 91% of the sun's radiant heat.
- Emittance (AS/NZS 4201.5:1994 / ASTM E408): antiglare outer face E0.09; silver inner face E0.05.
- Duty (Table 1, AS/NZS 4200.1:1994): Medium duty. Vapour barrier (ASTM E96): High. Water barrier (AS/NZS 4201.4:1994): High.
- Shrinkage (AS/NZS 4201.3:1994) ≤ 0.5%; passes dry and wet delamination (AS/NZS 4201.1/4201.2:1994); passes surface corrosion (AS/NZS 4859.1:2002); Flammability Index (AS 1530.2) Low (≤ 5).
- Total R-value performance example, brick veneer wall (110 mm brick / 40 mm reflective air gap / XP PLUS / 70 mm reflective air gap / 10 mm plasterboard, no bulk insulation): R<sub>T</sub> 1.6 summer, R<sub>T</sub> 1.9 winter.
- Thermal calculations per AS/NZS 4859.1:2002/Amdt.1; for upward-facing surfaces in non-vented cavities, Total R-value is calculated assuming slight dust cover per AS/NZS 4859.1 Paragraph K3.2(a)(ii); air gaps assumed still parallel air gaps and the membrane continuous.
- Total R-value is reduced by dust accumulation on upward-facing surfaces and in ventilated cavities.
- Install with the patterned antiglare surface facing outwards; fasteners at 300 mm centres; seal all overlaps and discontinuities for it to act as a draught and vapour control membrane; install per AS/NZS 4200.2.
- Electrical safety: contains conductive aluminium foil — must not contact or be in close proximity to electrical wiring; not to be installed horizontally (ceiling or sub-floor) in an existing structure due to wiring in framing.
- Roll size: 60 m x 1350 mm (81 m² per roll), product code 137041.

## Caveats

- Publish date 09/16 (September 2016), Revision Level A, Doc Ref B0206 — relatively old; check bradfordthermoseal.com.au for the current revision before quoting.
- The R<sub>T</sub> 1.6/1.9 figures are for the specific depicted brick veneer build-up with empty (reflective) cavities and no bulk insulation; adding bulk insulation or ventilating cavities changes the result.
- Rated "High" as a vapour barrier (ASTM E96), so condensation risk positioning applies in cooler climates; the datasheet notes performance depends on installation and environmental conditions.
