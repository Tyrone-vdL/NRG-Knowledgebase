# A&L ComfortSmart Hybrid — Thermally Broken Slimline Sash Windows (Product Launch Deck, V3)

## Summary

A&L Windows & Doors internal/trade launch presentation for the ComfortSmart Hybrid series — a standard aluminium frame with a patent-pending thermally broken sash/panel, positioned as the "Goldilocks" 7-Star solution between A&L's standard aluminium range and fully thermally broken ComfortSmart/ComfortSmart Ultra. It tabulates U-value/SHGC performance for A&L and competitor window series (Stegbar, Trend, Bradnams, Southern Star, AWS, Wideline, Dowell, MTM) across glazing upgrades, lists WERS codes with NatHERS proxy codes for energy raters, and gives the 2024–25 release timeline. Key performance ladder for the Hybrid awning sash: U3.2 double-glazed clear (Good), U2.5 single-coat low-E DG (Better), U2.2 double-coat low-E DG (Best), versus U3.5 for the standard aluminium sash.

## Key claims

- ComfortSmart Hybrid = **traditional aluminium frame with a thermally broken sash** (trademarked, patent pending, designed by A&L's RDE team). Rationale: standard product "sometimes just misses" 7 Stars; full thermal break is overkill/cost-prohibitive for many jobs.
- Hybrid awning sash performance ladder (U-value, from the pricing step-change slide): **Default aluminium sash U3.5 → Good (Hybrid, DG clear) U3.2 → Better (Hybrid, DG soft-coat low-E) U2.5 → Best (Hybrid, DG double-coat low-E) U2.2**. Sell price step change quoted as 0% / 10% / 25% relative to standard clear DG.
- A&L Standard series (cool climate, 28% glass comparison): Clear/Clear DG **U3.5 / SHGC 0.54**; single low-E **U2.8 / 0.42**; double low-E **U2.5 / 0.39**; best double **U2.5 / 0.41**; fully thermally broken **U1.8 / 0.40**; TB sash **U2.2 / 0.44**.
- Competitor benchmarks (Clear/Clear DG, U/SHGC): Stegbar Residential 4.0/0.56; Trend Synergy 4.1/0.56; Bradnams 3.7/0.43; Southern Star 100 3.8/0.58; AWS 516 3.6/0.50; Wideline Standard 4.0/0.58; Dowell Elite 3.9/0.58; MTM 4.6/0.54. Thermally broken competitors cluster at ~U2.0 (Stegbar/Trend 2.0/0.40, Bradnams 2.0/0.39, AWS 755 i89 2.1/0.43, Dowell Dbl H/C 1.8/0.46).
- WERS codes with U-value/SHGC and NatHERS proxy codes (because not all new WERS codes are loaded into the NatHERS library — energy raters must use proxy codes):
  - DG Clear (Good): Hybrid Awning Sash A&L-120-001 **U3.2 / 0.56** (proxy TND-038-003, 3.2/0.54); Fixed A&L-046-001 2.9/0.68; Sliding Door A&L-013-TBA 3.3/0.66 (proxy A&L-026-023 3.3/0.65); Boutique Door A&L-032-042 3.4/0.44.
  - DG Single Coat Low-E (Better): Hybrid Awning Sash A&L-120-002 **U2.5 / 0.44** (proxy TND-007-017, 2.5/0.46); Fixed A&L-046-301 1.9/0.53; Sliding Door 2.3/0.50; Boutique Door A&L-043-304 3.3/0.42.
  - DG Double Coat Low-E (Best): Hybrid Awning Sash A&L-120-006 **U2.2 / 0.39** (proxy STG-053-051, 2.2/0.40); Fixed A&L-046-304 1.6/0.48; Sliding Door 2.1/0.45; Boutique Door A&L-109-034 2.1/0.47.
  - Standard A&L DG baseline: Awning A&L-004-012 3.5/0.54; Boutique Awning A&L-024-012 3.8/0.49; Fixed A&L-046-001 2.9/0.68; Sliding Door A&L-013-004 3.5/0.63; Boutique Door A&L-032-001 4.0/0.54.
- Climate guidance: Victoria's main climate zones 6, 7 and 8 are heating climates — windows want a **low U-value and a high SHGC** (capture winter solar gain). Recommended 7-Star path in Victoria: ComfortSmart Hybrid DG clear (Good) → DG soft-coat low-E on surface 2 (Better) → DG double-coat low-E, soft + hard coatings (Best). For Queensland: heating climates use the Hybrid series; cooling climates use Standard & Boutique series with single-glazed hard-coat low-E (clear / neutral / grey).
- Series construction comparison: Standard slimline DG awning 18 mm glazing, 37 mm stile, 44 mm rail, 20 mm jamb; ComfortSmart Hybrid TB slimline sash same profile dimensions; ComfortSmart Ultra (TB semi-commercial) 20/24 mm glazing, 40 mm stile/rail, 35 mm sill/jamb.
- Release timeline: quoting slimline Hybrid sash available now (deck dated 2024); delivering slimline sash 1 Oct (2024); wideline sash 16 Dec (2024); sliding/stacker Hybrid panel quoting 16 Jan, delivering March 2025; extra colours (Woodland Grey, Jasper, Dune, Precious Silver) 1 Jan 2025. Launch colours: Black (matt), Monument (matt), White (gloss), Surfmist (satin).

## Caveats

- Sales/marketing deck, not a test certificate — U-values/SHGCs are quoted by A&L for a "cool climate, 28% glass" comparison scenario; use the WERS-listed values per product code for ratings.
- The deck itself flags that new WERS codes were not yet in the NatHERS library at publication (2024) and proxy codes were required — check current NatHERS library status before specifying.
- Competitor figures are A&L's characterisation of the market; verify independently before citing against a competitor.
- Slide-table extraction interleaved rows/columns; verify specific code/value pairs against the source PDF before quoting.
