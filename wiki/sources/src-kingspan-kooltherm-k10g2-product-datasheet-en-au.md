# Kingspan Kooltherm K10 G2 Soffit Board – Product Datasheet

## Summary

This is the Australian product datasheet (Issue 13, February 2024) for Kingspan Kooltherm K10 G2 Soffit Board, a rigid thermoset phenolic insulation designed for installation on concrete soffits in roof, floor, and sub-floor applications. The document covers thermal performance data, product specifications, fire ratings, standards compliance, and installation instructions. It is applicable to NCC Volume One Class 2–9 buildings and NCC Volume Two Class 1 and 10a buildings.

## Key claims

- Declared thermal conductivity (λ-value) is 0.022 W/m.K at 23°C for insulant thickness ≥ 45 mm, and 0.023 W/m.K at 23°C for insulant thickness 25–44 mm, tested to AS/NZS 4859.1:2018 / ASTM C518-2017.
- Declared product R-values range from R1.10 (25 mm) to R4.60 (100 mm); all values are at 23°C.
- Total R-values (building element) are provided for three applications with 150 mm concrete: roof with no ceiling, roof with suspended ceiling void greater than 300 mm, and unenclosed sub-floor — calculated in accordance with AS/NZS 4859.2:2018 and NZS 4214:2006.
- For a roof application with no ceiling (150 mm concrete), total R-values range from RT 2.0 (heat flow in) / RT 1.5 (heat flow out) at 25 mm, up to RT 5.3 (heat flow in) / RT 5.0 (heat flow out) at 100 mm.
- The product achieves NCC Group 2 fire classification, tested to AS 5637.1:2015 / AS ISO 9705:2003, with a SMOGRARC of ≤ 30 m²/s² x 1000.
- Early fire hazard testing to AS 1530.3:1999 returned a Spread of Flame Index of 0 and Smoke Development ≤ 3.
- The product holds FM Approval to FM 4880 Standard for ceiling/soffit installation (thicknesses 25 mm to 119 mm covered), valid only with non-combustible walls.
- The product is CodeMark-certified for NCC compliance and manufactured in Australia.
- Emittance of the foil face is E0.05, tested to ASTM C1371:2015.
- Compressive stress (insulant) exceeds 100 kPa at 10% compression on average, tested to AS 2498.3:1993.
- Water vapour transmission resistance is > 35 MN.s/g, tested to ASTM E96 Part B 2016.
- Standard board dimensions are 2400 mm × 1200 mm (2.88 m²); nominal thicknesses available are 25, 30, 40, 45, 50, 60, 70, 80, and 90 mm (product R-value table also lists 100 mm).
- A minimum of 11 insulation fasteners with a minimum head diameter of 35 mm per board is required for direct fixing to concrete soffits; this pattern is supported by FM Approvals.
- Board joints must be taped with 96 mm wide silver Kingspan Insulation Tape; exposed edges require a minimum 48 mm overlap of foil tape onto the board face.
- The product is warranted for 10 years for both residential and commercial installations, subject to the terms of the full warranty document.

## Caveats

- Datasheet is Issue 13, dated February 2024; product specifications may be amended without prior notice per Kingspan's disclaimer.
- Total R-value tables assume 150 mm concrete; results will differ for other slab thicknesses or construction configurations not covered in the document.
- FM Approval is valid for ceiling/soffit installation only with non-combustible walls; other configurations require separate confirmation from Kingspan Technical Services.
