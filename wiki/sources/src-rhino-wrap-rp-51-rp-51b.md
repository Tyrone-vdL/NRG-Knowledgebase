# Rhino Wrap RP-51 & RP-51B Reflective Wall Wrap Data Sheet

## Summary

Data sheet (Thor Building Products, October 2019) for Rhino Wrap RP-51 and RP-51B, heavy-duty reflective foil laminate wall wraps for residential and commercial walls — RP-51 is a Class 1 vapour barrier / water barrier, RP-51B is the Class 3 vapour-permeable "breather" variant, both with reflective emittance ≤ 0.05. States that up to R1.1 can be added to a wall system when installed behind brick veneer or other cladding with a drained cavity.

## Key claims

- RP-51: Vapour Classification Class 1 (Vapour Barrier); Water Control Classification: Water Barrier. RP-51B: Vapour Classification Class 3 (Vapour Permeable); Non-Water Barrier ("breather").
- Both heavy duty; emittance of reflective face max 0.05 (reflective ≤ 0.05); both electrically conductive.
- Water Vapour Transmission Rate: RP-51 max 2; RP-51B min 140 (units printed as "Ng/Ns" in the sheet — verify units against AS/NZS 4200.1 conventions).
- Flammability Index low, ≤ 5 in accordance with AS 1530.2; satisfies BCA Part 3.7.1 Fire Hazard Properties.
- Tensile strength: machine direction min 12.5 kN/m, lateral direction min 7.5 kN/m; edge tear resistance min 80 N both directions (both products).
- Complies with AS/NZS 4200.1 for pliable building membranes, recognised by BCA Part 3.5 Roof and Cladding; install per AS/NZS 4200.2.
- Wall installation claim: "Up to R1.1 may be added to the wall system" when installed as a pliable building membrane behind brick veneer / other claddings with a drained cavity (reflective airspace effect).
- A minimum 20 mm air space adjacent to the reflective foil face is recommended to achieve maximum insulation values.
- Construction: aluminium foil bonded to a blue high-strength reinforcing polymer mesh with flame-retardant adhesive (blue reduces reflective glare during installation).
- Roll sizes: 1350 mm × 30 m (40.5 m²), 1350 mm × 60 m (81 m²), 1500 mm × 30 m (45 m²), 1500 mm × 60 m (90 m²); shrinkage of up to 2% can be expected; no contact with wet concrete or mortar; clad over A.S.A.P.

## Caveats

- Published October 2019 — references BCA-era clause numbering (Part 3.5, Part 3.7.1), which predates the NCC 2022 restructure; map clauses to current NCC before citing for compliance.
- The "up to R1.1 added" figure is a manufacturer system claim dependent on a drained cavity and reflective airspace; it is not a material R-value and must not be applied without the required air gap.
- WVTR units as printed ("Ng/Ns") appear garbled in extraction; verify against the source PDF.
