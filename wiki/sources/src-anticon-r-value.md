# Bradford Anticon Roofing Blanket — R-Values and Technical Data

## Summary

Technical information extract for Bradford Anticon glasswool roofing blanket products, listing material R-values, thicknesses, roll dimensions, densities and pack weights for the Anticon, Acousticon and Agricon ranges. Also covers maximum service temperatures, moisture absorption and compliance with AS/NZS 4859.1. Useful when specifying roof blanket R-values in thermal assessments.

## Key claims

- Anticon 55 has a base blanket material R-value of R1.3 at 60 mm thickness.
- Anticon 75 has a material R-value of R1.8 at 80 mm thickness.
- Anticon 95 has a material R-value of R2.3 at 100 mm thickness.
- Anticon 110 has a material R-value of R2.5 at 110 mm thickness.
- Anticon 100HP has a material R-value of R2.5 at 100 mm thickness (higher density than Anticon 110).
- Anticon 130 has a material R-value of R3.0 at 130 mm thickness.
- Acousticon and Agricon are both R1.5 at 75 mm thickness.
- The R-values quoted are base blanket material R-values (R<sub>MAT</sub>), not Total R-values of the installed roof system.
- Maximum service temperature: glasswool base blanket 350°C; foil facing 70°C; Ultraphon facing 300°C.
- Moisture absorption for all products is less than 0.2% by volume (tested 4 days at 50°C and 95% relative humidity).
- All products comply with AS/NZS 4859.1 "Materials for the Thermal Insulation of Buildings".
- Nominal blanket widths are 1200 mm (Anticon 55 also available 1400 mm); roll lengths 10-15 m depending on product.
