# Platinum "R" Floor Brochure (Web)

## Summary

Marketing brochure for Platinum "R" Floor, an Australian-made all-in-one insulated structural flooring panel (plywood skins over a polystyrene foam core, a SIPS-type system) from Platinum Timber & Ply, combining structural flooring and underfloor insulation in one install with panel R-values from R1.2 to R2.5. Includes the R-value/weight table, acoustic test results (Alpha Acoustics) and the wet-area set-down detail.

## Key claims

- Panel R-values by thickness: 54 mm = R1.2 (40.9 kg panel), 64 mm = R1.5 (41.9 kg), 84 mm = R2.0 (43.9 kg), 99 mm = R2.5 (45.3 kg).
- Construction: Australian-produced structural plywood top and bottom skins over an Australian-manufactured foam core; the wet-area section drawing labels the core "extruded polystyrene".
- Standard sizes: 2400×1200×54 mm, 2400×1200×64 mm, 2400×1180×84 mm, 2400×1180×99 mm.
- Described as a Structural Insulated Panel System (SIPS) that exceeds Australian Standard load requirements by up to four times.
- Thermal and acoustic properties eliminate the need for additional thermal breaks (e.g. over steel joists); structural flooring and underfloor insulation installed in one process.
- Wet areas: no joist set-down required — the panel automatically provides the set-down; detail uses a 6 mm FC strip and 19 mm FC wet area flooring (JH Scyon or equivalent) with 5 mm adhesive beads.
- Acoustic tests (Doc J3256.1, Alpha Acoustics Pty Ltd, bedroom/garage source-receiver at 75 dB(A) source): bare panel over 300 mm joists with 10 mm plasterboard, no cavity insulation = 35 dBA floor noise reduction; carpet over = 36 dBA; carpet + R3 Earthwool cavity insulation = 40 dBA; carpet + 2 layers 13 mm sound-rated plasterboard on resilient mounts + R3 Earthwool = 55 dBA.
- Sustainably manufactured from plantation radiata pine, Responsible Wood Certified, 100% recyclable at end of use, Australian Made certified; termite treatment options available.
- Contact: Platinum Timber & Ply, 5 Aliciajay Circuit, Luscombe QLD 4207, ph 07 3803 7488, platinumtimber.com.au.
