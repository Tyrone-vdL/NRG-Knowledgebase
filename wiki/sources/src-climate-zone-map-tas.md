# ABCB Climate Zone Map — Tasmania (Zones 7 and 8, with LGA table)

## Summary

ABCB climate zone map for Tasmania (release Sep 2019, version VC00031.3) including an accessible table assigning every Tasmanian Local Government Area to NCC climate zone 7 or zones 7 and 8 — all of Tasmania falls in zones 7-8 for thermal design. The extracted file also contains labels from a Northern Territory climate zone map bundled in the same document.

## Key claims

- Table 1 "NCC climate zones for thermal design: Tasmania" assigns every Tasmanian LGA to zone 7, with some LGAs spanning zones 7 and 8 — no Tasmanian LGA is in zones 1-6.
- LGAs in climate zone 7 only: Brighton, Burnie, Circular Head, Clarence, Devonport, Flinders Island, Georgetown, Glamorgan/Spring Bay, Glenorchy, Hobart, Kingborough, King Island, Latrobe, Launceston, Sorell, Southern Midlands, Tasman, Waratah/Wynyard, West Tamar.
- LGAs spanning climate zones 7 and 8: Break O'Day, Central Coast, Central Highlands, Derwent Valley, Dorset, Huon Valley, Kentish, Meander Valley, Northern Midlands, West Coast.
- Release date Sep 2019, print version VC00031.3; this release added the table for web content accessibility (WCAG) and aligned styling with NCC 2019 branding. The previous version was last amended May 2009 (same version number VC00031.3).
- Published by the ABCB, © Commonwealth of Australia and the States and Territories 2019, under a Creative Commons Attribution-No Derivatives 4.0 International licence; developed from a Bureau of Meteorology map.
- The extracted text also includes a Northern Territory climate zone map (legend Zones 1-8; LGA labels including Darwin, Palmerston, Litchfield, Katherine, Alice Springs, Barkly, East Arnhem, MacDonnell, Tiwi Islands; towns including Tennant Creek, Yulara, Nhulunbuy, Jabiru) — apparently bundled in the same PDF, without an accompanying zone table.

## Caveats

- The map itself is visual content; only the legend, place labels and the Tasmanian LGA zone table survive text extraction. For zone boundaries within an LGA that spans zones 7 and 8, consult the original PDF (Z - NCC/_CLIMATE ZONE MAPS/ClimateZoneMapTAS.pdf) or abcb.gov.au.
- The Northern Territory content appears in the extraction without a zone table — NT zone assignments cannot be read from text; verify visually against the source PDF.
- Sep 2019 release aligned to NCC 2019 branding; confirm currency against the ABCB website before relying on it for current NCC compliance.
