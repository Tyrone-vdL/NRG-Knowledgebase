# Danpalon Polycarbonate Panels — Optical and Solar Properties (LT/ST/SR/SHGC)

## Summary

Danpalon (danpalon.com.au) data tables of optical and solar properties — light transmission (LT), solar transmission (ST), solar reflection (SR) and Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC) — for single-glazed polycarbonate panels (4, 8, 10, 12 and 16 mm in numerous colours), double-glazed exterior/interior panel combinations, and Softlite matt-finish variants. The key reference when an assessor needs SHGC for Danpalon roofing/walling glazing inputs.

## Key claims

- Definitions: LT = % visible light transmission (400-700 nm); ST = % total solar radiation transmission (300-2800 nm); SR = % total solar reflection (300-2800 nm); SHGC = total solar energy transmitted through the panel, calculated as %ST + 0.2 x [1 - (%ST + %SR)].
- DP4mm single glazing examples: Clear LT 89% / ST 80% / SR 17% / SHGC 0.81; Grey LT 41% / SHGC 0.58; Reflective Grey LT 20% / SHGC 0.28.
- DP8mm and DP10mm share most values: Clear LT 71% / ST 60% / SR 36% / SHGC 0.61; Opal LT 35% / SHGC 0.42; Dark Opal LT 20% / SHGC 0.27; Reflective Grey LT 20% / SHGC 0.28; Green LT 60% / SHGC 0.55; Ice LT 60% / SHGC 0.57.
- DP16mm single glazing examples: Clear LT 63% / SHGC 0.53; Ice LT 51% / SHGC 0.52; Opal LT 22% / SHGC 0.32; Reflective Grey LT 20% / SHGC 0.28.
- Double glazing combinations span SHGC 0.186 (DP10 Reflective Grey over DP8 Reflective Grey) up to 0.502 (DP10 Clear over DP8 Clear); e.g. DP10 Clear/DP8 Opal SHGC 0.352; DP16 Ice/DP16 Ice SHGC 0.336; DP16 Opal/DP16 Opal SHGC 0.214.
- Lowest single-panel SHGC listed is 0.27 (DP8/DP10 Dark Opal); lowest double-glazed SHGC listed is 0.186.
- Softlite (matt finish) variants: DP12mm Ice Softlite LT 56% / SHGC 0.57; DP16mm Ice Softlite LT 40% / SHGC 0.45; DP16mm Reflective Grey Softlite LT 20% / SHGC 0.28. Softlite nulls the "neon effect" for bright colours and can be added to any colour on one or both sides.
- Items marked with an asterisk are not stocked in Australia — 8-12 week lead times and production surcharges may apply.

## Caveats

- No publication date, test standard or certification body cited in the extract — values are manufacturer-published and the SHGC is from Danpalon's stated formula, not (e.g.) WERS/AFRC-rated window system values.
- Properties are for the panel only, not a complete glazed system with framing; whole-of-window SHGC/U-value will differ.
- DP16mm Dark Opal row has no values in the extract (blank in source or lost in extraction).
