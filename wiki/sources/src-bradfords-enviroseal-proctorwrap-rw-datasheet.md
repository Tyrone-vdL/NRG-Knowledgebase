# Bradford Enviroseal ProctorWrap RW — Vapour Permeable Wall Wrap Datasheet

## Summary

Technical datasheet for Bradford Enviroseal ProctorWrap RW, a Light Wall duty, Class 4 vapour permeable, non-reflective, water barrier wall wrap for residential brick veneer and lightweight clad walls. Covers AS/NZS 4200.1 classifications, condensation-control positioning (warm to cold climate zones), BAL suitability and Total R-value application tables for three wall types with R2.5 insulation.

## Key claims

- Classification per AS/NZS 4200.1: Light Wall duty; Water Control Classification (AS/NZS 4201.4) Water Barrier; Vapour Classification (ASTM E96) Class 4 Vapour Permeable.
- Vapour permeability (ASTM E96): > 1.1403 μg/N.s.
- Emissivity (AS 4201.5): Non-Reflective (0.9) on both inward and outward faces — this product provides no reflective R-value.
- Flammability Index (AS 1530.2): ≤ 5 (Low); Electrical Conductivity (AS/NZS 3100): Non-Conductive.
- Burst strength (AS 2001.2.19) ≥ 200 N; edge tear resistance (TAPPI T470) ≥ 90 machine and lateral; shrinkage (AS/NZS 4201.3) ≤ 0.5%; passes wet and dry delamination (AS/NZS 4201.1/4201.2).
- Suitable in BAL regions 12.5 to FZ in accordance with AS 3959.
- Recommended for warm to cold climate zones where higher insulation levels are used and condensation control is required (humidity higher inside than outside); NOT recommended in tropical climate zones where outside humidity exceeds inside.
- Application table Total R-values with R2.5 bulk insulation: brick veneer wall R<sub>T</sub> 2.8 (summer and winter); lightweight clad battened R<sub>T</sub> 2.7; lightweight clad direct fix R<sub>T</sub> 2.6.
- Construction: triple-layer polyolefin non-woven textile (spun bonded polyolefin / vapour permeable film / spun bonded polyolefin), calendared together; nominal thickness < 1.0 mm; RW-IT variant adds integrated tape release liners.
- Weather exposure limit: secondary sarking only — inspect for damage if exposed more than 6 weeks (walls) or 2 weeks (roof applications) before cladding.
- Roll sizes include 1500 mm x 30 m (45 m²), 1500 mm x 50 m (75 m²), 2750 mm x 25 m (68.75 m²) and 3000 mm x 25 m (75 m²).
- CSR/Bradford recommends designers run a condensation risk analysis and refers to the ABCB Handbook 'Condensation in Buildings'.
