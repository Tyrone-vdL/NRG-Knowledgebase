# Ametalin SilverFloor Reflective Underfloor Insulation — Technical Data Sheet (APM-23247-10)

## Summary

Manufacturer's technical data sheet for Ametalin SilverFloor (product code SF), a Heavy Duty, vapour permeable, double-sided reflective foil underfloor insulation for use under floors directly adhered to floor joists, giving Total System R-Values of RT 3.36 winter / RT 1.52 summer (enclosed unventilated sub-floor) and RT 2.05 winter / RT 0.77 summer (unenclosed ventilated sub-floor). The sheet covers construction (six-layer woven polypropylene with 97% reflective foil both sides), material classifications to AS/NZS 4200.1:2017 and AS/NZS 4859.1:2018, fire performance, dimensions and specification wording. Includes 5 mm weep holes at 400 mm intervals to let water and water vapour drain through, preventing moisture build-up under flooring.

## Key claims

- Total System R-Value, suspended framed floor with **enclosed, unventilated** sub-floor, SilverFloor installed between joists or over bearers: **Winter RT 3.36; Summer RT 1.52**.
- Total System R-Value, suspended framed floor with **unenclosed, ventilated** sub-floor: **Winter RT 2.05; Summer RT 0.77**.
- R-values apply to typical conditions for mainland Australian capital cities, calculated by an independent consulting engineer in accordance with **AS/NZS 4859.2:2018**; contribution depends on installation and environmental conditions.
- To achieve optimum thermal performance, a **≥ 90 mm air gap adjacent to the upper foil side** must be maintained.
- Emittance value: **0.03 printed side, 0.03 foil side** (AS/NZS 4201.5:1994); Emittance classification: IR Reflective, IR Reflective; Emittance category RR.
- Vapour control: **Class 3 Vapour Permeable**, vapour permeance **0.3009 µg/N·s** (ASTM E96). Water control: **Non-Water Barrier** (AS/NZS 4201.4:1994). Air control: **Non-Air Barrier**.
- Duty classification: **Heavy** per AS/NZS 4200.1:2017. Nominal thickness **0.13 mm**.
- Flammability Index: **Low (≤ 5)** tested to AS 1530.2-1993. Electrically conductive.
- Tensile strength: 12.8 kN/m machine direction (min 12.5), 14.2 kN/m lateral (min 7.5) per AS 1301.448s-91. Edge tear: 439 N MD / 452 N LD (min 80 N, TAPPI T 470 om-89). Shrinkage 0.0% (< 0.5% requirement).
- NCC compliance claim: complies with AS/NZS 4859.1:2018 and AS/NZS 4200.1:2017, therefore meets NCC requirements for insulation and sarking-type materials.
- Designed for floors with a sealed air space between flooring and enclosed sub-floor space; where the sub-floor is exposed to wind (e.g. pole construction), a lining should be installed under the floor joists.
- Weep holes: 5 mm at 400 mm intervals allow water and water vapour to permeate through.
- Roll dimensions: 500 mm x 60 m (30 m²) and 1500 mm x 60 m (90 m²).
- Install in accordance with AS 4200.2:2017 (Pliable Building Membranes and Underlays, Part 2: Installation). Store away from alkaline products, cement and mortar.
