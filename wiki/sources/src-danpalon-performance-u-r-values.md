# Danpalon Single Panels — U-Values and R-Values

## Summary

Single-table Danpalon datasheet listing U-values (W/m²·°C) and R-values (m²·°C/W) for five single polycarbonate panel types from 4 mm solid wall to 16 mm multicell. Thicker multicell panels roughly halve the U-value of the 4 mm solid sheet (5.36 down to 1.53).

## Key claims

- Danpalon 4mm Solid Wall: U-value 5.36 W/m²·°C; R-value 0.1865 m²·°C/W.
- Danpalon 8mm Honeycomb: U-value 2.46 W/m²·°C; R-value 0.4065 m²·°C/W.
- Danpalon 10mm Honeycomb: U-value 2.11 W/m²·°C; R-value 0.4739 m²·°C/W.
- Danpalon 12mm Multicell: U-value 1.84 W/m²·°C; R-value 0.5434 m²·°C/W.
- Danpalon 16mm Multicell: U-value 1.53 W/m²·°C; R-value 0.6535 m²·°C/W.
- Values are for single panels only.

## Concepts touched

glazing, roofing, polycarbonate, U-value, R-value

## Caveats

- No test standard, test conditions or publication date stated — manufacturer-published panel-only figures.
- Panel-centre values, not whole-of-system (framed/installed) U-values; pair with the companion Danpalon optical/solar properties sheet for SHGC.
