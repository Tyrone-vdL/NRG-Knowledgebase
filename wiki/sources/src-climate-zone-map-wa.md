# NCC Climate Zone Map — Western Australia (incl. NT panel)

## Summary

ABCB climate zone map and lookup tables assigning every Western Australian Local Government Area (LGA) to an NCC climate zone for thermal design, including separate tables for Perth urban and Perth surrounds, plus a Northern Territory map panel. Released September 2019 (Version VC00031.3), developed from a Bureau of Meteorology map. This is the authoritative reference for which NCC climate zone applies to a WA project site — the input that determines which insulation, glazing and fabric tables apply under the NCC.

## Key claims

- All of Perth urban LGAs (Perth, Stirling, Fremantle, Joondalup-adjacent inner metro, Bayswater, Belmont, Canning, Melville, Cottesloe, Subiaco, Vincent, etc.) are **climate zone 5**.
- Perth surrounds: coastal/plain LGAs (Armadale, Wanneroo, Joondalup, Rockingham, Kwinana, Mandurah, Swan, Mundaring, Kalamunda, Gosnells, Cockburn, Bunbury, Busselton, Harvey, Murray, Waroona, Serpentine-Jarrahdale, Gingin, Chittering, Dandaragan, Capel, Dardanup, Collie) are **zone 5**; wheatbelt/inland LGAs (York, Northam, Narrogin, Beverley, Brookton, Toodyay, Goomalling, Pingelly, Quairading, Cunderdin, Kellerberrin, Katanning, Moora, etc.) are **zone 4**; southern forest LGAs (Boyup Brook, Bridgetown-Greenbushes, Donnybrook-Balingup, Kojonup, Broomehill/Tambellup) are **zone 6**.
- Kimberley and Pilbara coastal LGAs (Broome, Derby-West Kimberley, Wyndham-East Kimberley, Port Hedland, Roebourne, Exmouth) are **climate zone 1**.
- East Pilbara spans **zones 1 and 3**; Halls Creek, Ashburton, Carnarvon, Shark Bay and Upper Gascoyne are **zone 3**.
- Goldfields/mid-west interior LGAs (Meekatharra, Wiluna, Leonora, Laverton, Coolgardie, Cue, Yalgoo, Sandstone, Menzies, Mount Magnet, Murchison, Ngaanyatjarraku, Merredin and most wheatbelt shires) are **zone 4**; Kalgoorlie/Boulder and Dundas span **zones 4 and 5**.
- South coast: Albany, Denmark, Cranbrook, Plantagenet, Gnowangerup and Manjimup are **zone 6**; Esperance and Ravensthorpe span **zones 5 and 6**; Jerramungup spans 5 and 6.
- City of Greater Geraldton and Chapman Valley are **zone 4**; Dongara (Irwin) and Northampton coastal strip include **zone 5**; Augusta-Margaret River and Nannup are **zone 5**.
- Some LGAs are explicitly assigned two zones (e.g. Esperance 5, 6; Kalgoorlie/Boulder 4, 5; East Pilbara 1, 3; Northampton 4, 5; Dundas 4, 5; Jerramungup 5, 6) — zone must be resolved from the map at the specific site.
- The document also contains the Northern Territory climate zone map panel (Darwin region zone 1 areas through to Alice Springs/MacDonnell zone 3 interior), with the same zone 1–8 legend.
- Published by ABCB under Commonwealth copyright; release date September 2019, Version VC00031.3 (previous amendment January 2013); for further information refer to ncc.abcb.gov.au.

## Caveats

- Release date September 2019 — confirm against the current ABCB climate zone map on ncc.abcb.gov.au before relying on a zone assignment, as zone boundaries are occasionally revised.
- Where an LGA is listed with two zones, the table alone cannot resolve the zone for a specific address — the map (or the NCC online lookup) must be consulted.
- PDF text extraction interleaved map labels with table rows; the LGA-to-zone assignments above are taken from the unambiguous tables, but a handful of entries show OCR noise (e.g. "Waging" for Wagin, "Marembeen" for Narembeen) — verify spelling-sensitive lookups against the source PDF.
