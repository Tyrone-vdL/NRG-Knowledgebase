# NCC Resource Kit — BCA Volumes One and Two Read Me (2016)

## Summary

A two-page 2016 cover note for the ABCB's NCC Resource Kit on BCA Volumes One and Two, explaining who the kit is for and how to deliver it. It is orientation material only — it describes the kit's components and recommends delivery approaches for educators and trainers.

## Key claims

- The kit provides educational resources for teaching professionals, industry association trainers, or employers to deliver an introduction and overview of NCC Volumes One and Two.
- The kit can be delivered standalone, but the ABCB recommends having access to the NCC (online or hardcopy) alongside; the NCC is free online via registration at www.abcb.gov.au.
- Components include FAQs, Case Studies and a main PowerPoint presentation; trainers are encouraged to tailor delivery (e.g. hide slides, present only relevant topics) for introductory vs intermediate audiences.
- Published 2016 under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 licence by the Commonwealth, States and Territories.

## Concepts touched

NCC, training

## Caveats

- 2016 administrative cover note with no technical content; the free-online-NCC claim remains true but the registration process and site have changed since.
- Recommended topic lists in the original are images/diagrams that did not survive text extraction.
