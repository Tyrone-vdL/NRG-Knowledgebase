# Ametalin ThermalBreak 8 Plus Reflective Foam Insulation / Thermal Break — Technical Data Sheet (DTE-43313-1)

## Summary

Manufacturer's technical data sheet for Ametalin ThermalBreak 8 Plus (product code TB8-30), an 8 mm XPE foam-core, double-sided reflective insulation that is a three-in-one reflective insulation, thermal break and medium vapour barrier for roofs, walls and floors — with material R-value R0.21 uncompressed / R0.20 compressed in-situ, meeting the NCC R0.2 thermal break requirement for steel-framed construction. Declares total system R-values (winter/summer) for eight typical roof and wall systems, plus material classifications to AS/NZS 4200.1:2017, BAL ratings, and detailed installation requirements for roofs, framed walls, double brick cavity walls and floors.

## Key claims

- **Material R-value: R0.21 (0.21 m²·K/W, ASTM C518) uncompressed; R0.20 compressed in-situ** — "meets NCC requirements for a thermal break of R0.2 in steel framed construction"; also suitable for timber-framed construction.
- Construction: 8.0 mm chemically cross-linked closed-cell high-density XPE foam core; foil laminate **emissivity 0.03 (bright side) and 0.05 (anti-glare side)**.
- Declared Total System R-Values for typical systems (Winter RT / Summer RT, per the datasheet table):
  - Metal roof, unventilated, 22° pitched, 190 mm raked ceiling (calc ref 12239): **RT 1.4 / RT 3.7**
  - Metal roof, ventilated, 22° pitched with flat ceiling (calc ref 12335/299r404): **RT 1.3 / RT 2.8**
  - Metal roof, unventilated, 22° pitched with flat ceiling (calc ref 299r405): **RT 1.5 / RT 2.5**
  - Tile roof, unventilated, 22° pitched with flat ceiling (calc ref 12164): **RT 1.5 / RT 2.5**
  - Commercial office roof, suspended ceiling at 1000 mm (calc ref 299r381): **RT 1.4 / RT 4.6**
  - Warehouse shed roof, 5° metal roof, 100 mm ceiling (calc ref 299r380): **RT 1.5 / RT 3.2**
  - Warehouse shed roof, 5° metal roof, no ceiling (calc ref 299r382): **RT 1.0 / RT 2.0**
  - Steel stud framed wall, metal cladding direct to 90 mm stud, no lining (calc ref 299w44): **RT 1.3 / RT 1.1**
- Wall system graphics also show (Winter RT / Summer RT): stud framed **RT 1.9 / 1.7** (calc ref 299w510), brick veneer **RT 2.2 / 1.9** (calc ref 299w3811b), double brick **RT 2.3 / 2.1** (calc ref 299w371).
- System R-values calculated for typical mainland Australian capital city conditions to **AS/NZS 4859.1:2002/Amdt 1:2006**; values reduce with dust on upward-facing surfaces and in ventilated cavities.
- Vapour control: **Class 2 (Medium)**; vapour permeance **0.0371 µg/N·s** (ASTM E96); vapour resistance **64.89 MN·s/g**; WVT rate **1.92 g/m²·24 hr at 23°C, 50% RH**. Water control: **Water Barrier**.
- Duty: **Extra Heavy** (AS/NZS 4200.1:2017). Flammability Index **Low (≤ 5)** (AS 1530.2-1993). Electrically conductive. Tensile strength 21.4 kN/m MD / 19.4 kN/m LD; edge tear 1078 N MD / 939 N LD.
- Bushfire: complies with **AS 3959-2009** for roof systems **BAL-LOW to BAL-40** and wall systems **BAL-LOW to BAL-FZ**.
- Dimensions: 1350 mm x 22.25 m (30 m²) **plus 150 mm overlap flap**; thickness 8 mm; nominal weight 17.9 kg.
- Installation: as thermal control membrane, ensure **air gap ≥ 20 mm to the low-emittance side**; roof joins overlapped ≥ 150 mm (or ≥ 50 mm taped); under tiles, drape ≤ 40 mm below battens with anti-ponding device per AS 4200.2:2017; cut back 50 mm clear of hot flues; foil must not contact wet concrete/mortar (alkali corrosion); within 500 m of the sea foil surfaces should face an enclosed un-vented air space.
- Reflective insulation note (cross-document): the NCC handbook states reflective insulation alone is not suitable as a thermal break where it requires an adjoining airspace — ThermalBreak 8 Plus addresses this with a foam core providing R0.20 in-situ without an airspace.
