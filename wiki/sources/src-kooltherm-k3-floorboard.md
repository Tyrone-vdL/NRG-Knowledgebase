# Kingspan Kooltherm K3 Floorboard — Under-Slab Floor Insulation Datasheet

## Summary

Kingspan datasheet (Issue 4, March 2016) for Kooltherm K3 Floorboard, a rigid thermoset phenolic insulation board laid below ground-bearing concrete floor slabs, with thermal conductivity 0.020–0.021 W/m·K and product R-values from R1.2 (25 mm) to R2.5 (50 mm). Includes Total Construction R-values for a 150 mm slab profile and the under-slab installation sequence (DPM placement, loose-laid break-bonded boards, polythene separation layer).

## Key claims

- Thermal conductivity (λ-value): 0.020 W/m·K for insulant thickness ≥ 45 mm; 0.021 W/m·K for 25–44 mm.
- Product R-values: 25 mm = R1.2; 30 mm = R1.4; 40 mm = R1.9; 50 mm = R2.5.
- Total Construction R-values (heat flow out), through a profile with 150 mm concrete slab: 25 mm = RT1.5; 30 mm = RT1.7; 40 mm = RT2.2; 50 mm = RT2.8. Contribution depends on installation and environmental conditions.
- Note the 7 Star Quick Guides cite 50 mm K3 as R2.3 in their floor build-up — slightly below this datasheet's R2.5 product value; treat the discrepancy as version/rounding drift and verify with Kingspan for current jobs.
- Construction: fibre-free closed-cell phenolic core with tissue-based facing on top and reflective aluminium foil facing underneath; board size 2270 mm × 1200 mm (2.72 m²); thicknesses 25, 30, 40, 50 mm.
- Compressive strength (BS EN 826): typically exceeds 100 kPa at 10% compression; un-reinforced floor screeds usable in most applications; not to be placed under direct point loads, foundations or slab thickenings.
- Water vapour resistance (BS EN 12086) > 100 MN·s/g; resistant to the passage of water vapour.
- Fire (AS 1530.3): 0/0/0/0-1; also Class 0 to BS 476 Parts 6 and 7 (UK classification, insulation core only).
- Compliant with AS/NZS 4859.1.
- Installation: must be laid over a damp-proof membrane (minimum 300 micron / 1200 gauge polythene), never in direct contact with subsoil; boards loose-laid break-bonded with joints lightly butted; two layers must be offset; foil face laid down to avoid glare; overlay with polythene separation sheet (minimum 125 micron / 500 gauge, 150 mm taped overlaps) before pouring the slab.
- Where vertical slab edge insulation is used, the DPM goes on the external face of the K3 Floorboard; protect boards from wheeled/foot traffic during installation.
- Zero ODP, low-GWP CFC/HCFC-free blowing agent; 10-year warranty residential and commercial; wet, damaged or MEK-solvent-contacted boards must not be used.

## Caveats

- Issue 4 dated March 2016; specification text cites Kingspan Insulation Limited (UK) management systems and BS test standards — check the current Australian datasheet for updated values and AS/NZS 4859.1:2018 conformance.
- Total R-values are for the stated 150 mm slab profile with heat flow out only; other slab thicknesses or heat-flow directions are not covered.
