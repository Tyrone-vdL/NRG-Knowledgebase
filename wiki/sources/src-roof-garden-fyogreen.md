# Fytogreen Roof Gardens General Specifications

## Summary

General specifications brochure (Fytogreen Australia, updated 06/2018) for green roof systems — extensive roof gardens (140-200 mm profile), intensive roof gardens/podiums/planter boxes (200-1500 mm+), and sloping roofs to 45° using the Geoweb cellular confinement system — including full layer build-ups and saturated weight guides. Positioned for "acoustic and temperature insulation, water management and aesthetic beauty" but contains no quantified R-values; its hard numbers are structural (saturated weights per m²) and hydraulic.

## Key claims

- Extensive roof gardens (140-200 mm profile): thin growing medium < 200 mm planted with predominantly native vegetation; all Fytogreen roof gardens are irrigated and designed for low maintenance.
- Extensive saturated weight guide: 140 mm total depth = 139 kg/m², 150 mm = 149 kg/m², 160 mm = 158 kg/m², 170 mm = 168 kg/m², 200 mm = 198 kg/m², 250 mm = 247 kg/m²; water at field capacity 38-74 L/m² across that range; plants allowance 25 kg/m².
- Extensive layer build-up: LDPE protection layer (min 200 micron); 20 mm Atlantis Flo-Cell 20 HDPE drainage cell (200 kPa crush strength, holds 1.8 L/m²); Bidim A14-A24 geofabric (2 mm, 360 gsm wet); Hydrocell 40 Extensive media (saturated weight guide 11 kg/m² per 10 mm of depth, saturated bulk density 980 kg/m³); 20 mm stone mulch; subsurface drip irrigation (Netafim Techline AS, 150 mm emitter spacing, 1 L/emitter/hr, 400 mm row spacing).
- Intensive roof gardens/podiums (245-945 mm total depth): total saturated weights 170 kg/m² (245 mm) to 975 kg/m² (945 mm); soil mix from 115 kg/m² at 100 mm to 920 kg/m² at 800 mm (large trees to 12 m); intensive media mix saturated bulk density 1150 kg/m³ (VIC/SA mix).
- Hydrocell RG-30 water reservoir layer: urea aldehyde resin hardfoam, dry weight 4 kg/m² per 100 mm, field capacity weight up to 55 kg/m² per 100 mm (up to 51 L/m² water), ~99% usable media volume; available 30/60/100 mm depths.
- Intensive media guide: Hydrocell 40 Lightweight Planter Media at 12 kg/m² per 10 mm of depth saturated weight allowance.
- Sloping roofs: Geoweb cellular confinement supports green roofs on slopes from 15° up to 45° (cell depths 75-200 mm); case study — Victorian Desalination Plant, Wonthaggi, 26,000 m² green roof (Australia's largest), with 650 m² on a 20° slope using 150 mm cells and 10 kN/m tendons.
- Waterproof membranes are explicitly excluded — "work by others"; the parapet should sit a minimum 20 mm above the finished garden level.
- Growing media mixes vary by state (e.g. VIC extensive: 40% Hydrocell flakes, 35% 10-14 mm scoria, 15% composted organics, 10% <7 mm scoria; NSW/QLD podium: 45% clinker ash, 40% Hydrocell flakes, 15% composted organics).

## Caveats

- Updated 06/2018; manufacturer specification with a disclaimer recommending user trials — no NCC clause references and no quantified R-value or thermal test data anywhere in the document.
- Saturated weight tables had degraded layout in extraction; verify load figures against the source PDF before structural use.
- Waterproofing and its certification are outside Fytogreen's supply — thermal/moisture compliance of the roof assembly must be resolved separately.
