# Q: What's the R-value of a 200mm Hebel wall?

## Answer

It depends which Hebel product and the moisture content. From the CSR Hebel Technical Manual (Jan 2006), Table 3.1:

| Product | R dry | R at 5% MC | R at 10% MC |
|---|---|---|---|
| Thermoblok 200mm | **1.72** | 1.43 | 1.23 |
| Sonoblok 200mm | 1.21 | 1.01 | 0.86 |
| Wall (Sound Barrier) 200mm | 1.45 | 1.21 | 1.03 |
| Floor 200mm | 1.37 | 1.14 | 0.98 |

**For typical wall construction, Thermoblok is the relevant product** — R 1.72 m²·K/W in the dry state, R 1.43 at 5% moisture.

## Important caveats

1. **This is the AAC fabric R-value only** — not the total wall R. To get total wall R-value, add the cavity (R_as), surface air films (R_s on both sides), and any cladding/lining/insulation layers.
2. **Source is dated January 2006** — confirm against current CSR Hebel literature before quoting in a client report.
3. **Moisture content matters** — a freshly built wall can sit above 10% MC for months. Most NCC compliance assumes dry state.

## What this likely doesn't get you on its own

A bare 200mm Thermoblok wall at R=1.72 (dry) is **below most NCC 2022 DTS wall R-value targets** for residential, especially in cooler climate zones (CZ 6, 7, 8). Plan on pairing it with a reflective wrap or additional insulation layer to hit 7-star.
