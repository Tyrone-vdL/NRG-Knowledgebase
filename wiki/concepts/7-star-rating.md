# 7-Star NatHERS Rating

NCC 2022 raised the minimum residential thermal performance from 6-star to **7-star NatHERS** (with the corresponding Whole-of-Home requirement). This is the design constraint that drives most of NRG's consulting work.

## Why this page exists

Almost every project NRG advises on has the same root question: *"What does it take to get this dwelling to 7 stars?"* The answer is climate-zone-specific and depends on fabric, glazing, orientation, and shading.
