# Sustainable Design Assessment (SDA) & Water Sensitive Urban Design (WSUD)

Council-level sustainability requirements that sit on top of NCC and NatHERS — common in Victoria, especially in Melbourne metro councils.

## Why this page exists

Many Melbourne projects need an SDA report and a WSUD response in addition to the energy rating. NRG sometimes prepares or coordinates these.

## Key sources

1. `Brimbank ESD Guidance - Water Sensitive Urban Design.PDF` — Brimbank Council's ESD guidance
2. `08 Stormwater Management Plan.pdf` — example stormwater management plan
3. `Keeping-our-stormwater-clean-builders-guidelines.pdf` — builder-facing guidelines
