# BASIX — Building Sustainability Index (NSW)

BASIX is NSW's standalone sustainability assessment scheme for new dwellings. NSW does not use NCC's energy efficiency provisions directly — BASIX is the parallel pathway.

## Why this page exists

NSW projects are not under NCC Part J/3.12 the same way other states are. Anyone working on a NSW dwelling needs to understand BASIX targets, the Thermal Performance Protocol, and the energy/thermal calculator.

## Key sources

1. `BASIX+Thermal+Performance+Protocol_June+2024-2.pdf` — **PRIMARY** — current protocol (June 2024)
2. `Energy+target+and+thermal+loads+calculator_V4_21+09+2023+1+v1.xlsx` — the calculator NRG uses
3. `THERMAL LOAD CAPS.png` — quick-reference image of thermal load caps

## Three BASIX assessments

- **Thermal comfort** (NatHERS-equivalent path or DTS path)
- **Energy** (regulated appliances / renewables — broadly WoH-aligned)
- **Water** (rainwater, fixtures — not the focus of NRG's energy work but part of BASIX)
