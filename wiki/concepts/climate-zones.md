# Australian Climate Zones (NCC)

The NCC defines 8 climate zones across Australia. Energy efficiency requirements (insulation R-values, glazing, shading) are climate-zone specific.

## Quick reference

| Zone | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Hot humid summer, warm winter | Darwin, Cairns |
| 2 | Warm humid summer, mild winter | Brisbane, Townsville |
| 3 | Hot dry summer, warm winter | Alice Springs |
| 4 | Hot dry summer, cool winter | Mildura |
| 5 | Warm temperate | Sydney, Perth, Adelaide |
| 6 | Mild temperate | Melbourne, Hobart (coastal) |
| 7 | Cool temperate | Canberra, Hobart (inland) |
| 8 | Alpine | Cooma, Thredbo |

*(Verify against `ClimateZoneMapAUST.pdf` for the authoritative map.)*
