# National Construction Code (NCC)

The NCC is Australia's national building code, published by the ABCB. Energy efficiency provisions sit within Volume One (commercial — Class 2-9) and Volume Two (residential — Class 1 & 10), with the **Housing Provisions** standard called up by Volume Two.

## NCC versions in this library

- **NCC 2019** — superseded but still relevant for older approvals (`PN - EE-04-Alterations-to-existing-Class-1 - NCC 2019.pdf`, `QDC 4.1 - NCC 2019 5STAR DTS.pdf`)
- **NCC 2022** — current — 7-star + WoH minimum (most files in `Z - NCC/`)
- **NCC 2025 PCD** — Public Comment Draft — coming into force soon (`NCC 2025 PCD - Volume Two-2.pdf`, `NCC 2025 PCD - ABCB Housing Provisions V1.1.pdf`)

**ALWAYS verify which NCC version a question is about.** Don't quote a 2022 rule for a 2019 project.
