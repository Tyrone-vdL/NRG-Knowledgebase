# Specification 44

Specification 44 is the NCC 2022 specification that allows a **first-principles performance solution** for energy efficiency — the path you take when DTS or NatHERS-only won't get the design over the line.

## Why this page exists

When a dwelling fails 7 stars by NatHERS but the design intent is fixed (heritage, solar passive trade-offs, unusual geometry), Spec 44 is the documented route to demonstrate equivalent performance via reference-building comparison.

## When to use Spec 44 (vs straight NatHERS)

- Design fails NatHERS but cannot be modified easily (heritage, site, owner preference)
- Unusual building geometry that NatHERS-accredited software handles poorly
- Mixed-use or non-standard occupancy
